Homecoming Week

Day: February 2-7, 2026

Kick off the spring semester with Homecoming Week 2026, from February 2-7, under the theme "Under the Blue & Gold Top." The week features exciting events including banner and talent contests, bonfires, live performances, and culminates in a tailgate celebrating the Homecoming King and Queen. Join in for a celebration full of spirit, competition, and Lander pride!
